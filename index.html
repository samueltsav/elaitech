About Us

Elai-Tech & Construction is a trusted name in Nigeria’s electrical and civil engineering industry. Based in Nyanya, Abuja, we specialize in domestic and industrial electrical installations, the supply of high-quality electrical equipment, and a wide range of general construction works, including roads, bridges, and infrastructure development.With over two decades of professional experience, Elai-Tech & Construction has built a strong reputation for reliability, technical excellence, and customer satisfaction. Our team of skilled engineers and technicians is committed to delivering safe, efficient, and durable solutions tailored to meet each client’s unique needs. At Elai-Tech & Construction, we combine expertise, innovation, and integrity to power progress and build lasting connections — one project at a time.
